About Us

Empowering Lives Through Comprehensive Rehabilitation

ROYAL VISION is a legally registered, voluntary service organisation dedicated for the upliftment of the visually impaired, blind, children with Intellectual Disabled and persons with multiple disabilities in all supporting means since 2006. It has all legal amenities such as Full Tax Exemption to Donors u/s 80G of Indian Income Tax and Sec. 12A(a). FCRA Registration also provides us to accept donations from Foreign Contribution through Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt of India.

Our Programmes

Special School for Intellectual Disabled

We are running a “Special School for Children with Intellectual Disabled” which facilitates the productive rehabilitation services to the children with Intellectual Disabled. This school is running with 48 children (4yrs to 17yrs) with multiple disabilities and cared by a team of 6 special teachers, 2 care takers and a physiotherapist.

Nurturing Growth for Intellectually Disabled Children: Providing tailored education to empower and support unique abilities.

Inclusive Learning for Special Needs Children: Fostering skills and confidence through individualized teaching methods.

Hostel for the Persons with Disabilities

Royal Vision has established a special Hostel for Visually Challenged persons by 2007. About 45 and more visually challenged students from in and around of the districts are accommodated in the hostel.

Safe and Supportive Living for Persons with Disabilities: Providing accessible facilities and a nurturing environment for growth.

Empowering Independence Through Inclusive Hostel Services: Ensuring comfort, care, and opportunities for persons with disabilities.

Audio Library

Audio Library For enhancing the skill based development and to access the quality lifestyle of the visually impaired, our ROYALVISION established a special Audio Library in Madurai district for visually impaired persons by the year 2012 and over the past 3 more years it has been served several visually impaired persons successfully.

Accessing Knowledge Through Audio for the Visually Impaired: Offering a wide range of books and resources in accessible audio formats.

Empowering Blind Persons with Audio Learning Tools: Providing easy access to education and entertainment through audio media.

Community Based Rehabilitation Center

Royalvision has been running a Community Based Rehabilitation program for the welfare of the persons with disabilities in all kinds. The purpose of the CBR program is to facilitate our service performances to the unreached beneficiaries located in other parts of the district.

Empowering Communities Through Inclusive Rehabilitation: Supporting persons with disabilities with local resources and services.

CBR: Bridging Gaps for Disability Inclusion: Promoting self-reliance and social integration within communities.

Resource and Rehabilitation Center

Royal Vision is running resource and rehabilitation center in Madurai district for the welfare of the persons with disabilities in all kinds. At present more than 15000 differently abled persons are benefited directly and indirectly through this center.

Empowering Lives with Holistic Rehabilitation Services: Offering therapy, training, and support for independent living in 70 characters.

Building a Brighter Future Through Resource Accessibility: Providing tools and resources to enhance skills and improve quality of life.

Special Coaching Classes on Computer & Group Exams

An Intensive Special Recruitment Drive Coaching Classes was conducted by the NATIONAL FEDERATION OF THE BLIND by the year 2015 for providing Special Training/ Coaching Classes to empower Visually Impaired / Blind persons to attend the Group Level Examinations. Our ROYALVISION played a major role as Training Facilitator under the National Federation of the Blind,Tamilnadu Branch, Madurai Unit.

Enhancing Skills Through Computer Coaching: Offering tailored classes to build digital expertise and confidence.

Preparing for Success in Group Exams: Providing focused training to excel in competitive exams and careers.

Family Counseling, Legal Aid and RTI Awareness

At ROYALVISION, we recognize the importance of holistic support for families and individuals facing challenges,Through Legal Aid, we offer assistance to those navigating complex legal situations, ensuring access to justice for marginalized and underprivileged communities,Our RTI Awareness Programs empower individuals by educating them about their rights to information under the Right to Information Act.

Strengthening Families Through Counseling Support: Providing guidance to resolve conflicts and enhance family relationships.

Empowering Communities with Legal Aid and RTI Awareness: Ensuring justice and transparency through legal assistance and RTI education.

Medical & Relief Fund

The Royal Vision Medical & Relief Fund is dedicated to providing financial assistance to individuals facing medical hardships and unforeseen emergencies. Our mission is to support those in need by offering timely aid to alleviate financial burdens during challenging times.

Medical Assistance: Financial support for medical treatments, surgeries, and essential medications.

Emergency Support: Assistance for unforeseen emergencies such as accidents or sudden illnesses.

Special Welfare Program

The Royal Vision Special Welfare Program is an initiative by Royal Vision, a legally registered non-profit public charitable organization dedicated to uplifting visually impaired individuals, children with intellectual disabilities, and persons with multiple disabilities.

Empowerment: To empower visually impaired individuals and those with multiple disabilities through education, vocational training, and rehabilitation services.

Support: To provide necessary support and resources to enhance the quality of life for the differently-abled community.

Helen Keller – The White Cane Day Celebration

Royalvision is regularly celebrated the Birthday of Dr. Ms. Helen Keller who has dedicated her life for the welfare of the persons with disabilities. To create awareness among the public about the issues related to the visually handicapped and the importance of community caring to the visually impaired, our Royalvision observed “White Cane Day” every year.

Celebrating White Cane Day for Empowering the Visually Impaired: Honoring independence and achievements of the visually impaired community.

Helen Keller’s Legacy: Inspiring Through White Cane Day: Raising awareness and advocating for equal opportunities for the blind.
