Royal Vision

ROYAL VISION is a legally registered, voluntary service organisation dedicated for the upliftment of the visually impaired, blind, children with Intellectual Disabled and persons with multiple disabilities in all supporting means since 2006. It has all legal amenities such as Full Tax Exemption to Donors u/s 80G of Indian Income Tax and Sec. 12A(a). FCRA Registration also provides us to accept donations from Foreign Contribution through Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt of India.
